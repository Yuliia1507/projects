What I do

With more than 1 year of experience as a front-end developer, I specialize in converting Figma designs into responsive and visually appealing websites.

My work involves:

Responsive Design:

Crafting websites that look great and function seamlessly on any device, from desktops to mobile phones.

Interactive User Experiences:

Building dynamic and engaging user experiences using JavaScript.

Cross-Browser Compatibility:

Making sure that websites work consistently across all major browsers and platforms.

Animation and Transitions:

Implementing smooth animations and transitions to enhance user interaction and provide a polished look and feel.

Collaboration and Communication:

Working closely with designers, back-end developers, and other stakeholders to deliver cohesive and high-quality projects.
